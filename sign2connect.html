    This demo uses MediaPipe Hands to track 21 hand landmarks live, then applies geometric rules to guess these letters and gestures. Letters requiring motion (J, Z) or fine orientation cues (C, G, H, K, M, N, P, Q, R, T, X) aren't included yet — a trained model would be needed for full alphabet accuracy. Some fist-shaped letters (A, E, S) may occasionally get confused with each other.
